MyFleetBuy-EV

EV Logistics and Planning

Electric Vehicles (EVs) present an opportunity to substantially lower the fueling, operating, and maintenance costs of your fleet - but they come with their own unique challenges.

With MyFleetBuy-EV's powerful planning and analysis capabilities, unlock the cost savings of EVs for your fleet while minimizing the risks and logistical challenges of range, charging, and more.

Range confidence

Worried that EVs won't have the range needed for your fleet's needs? Not sure how their longer recharge time will affect your operations? Worried that a vehicle will be stuck out of service if a driver forgets to plug in? MyFleetBuy-EV forecasts the range and battery usage of any EV for your fleet's own duty cycles.

With MyFleetBuy-EV, eliminate range anxiety and procure EVs with confidence.

Assess changing needs

Thinking of buying EVs but not sure how and where to charge? Wondering if you need to install new charging stations? How many? What kind? At which of your fleet's facility locations?

MyFleetBuy-EV helps assess your fleet's charging needs so you can procure EVs and charging stations with confidence.

Compare costs of charging vs. fueling

Electricity costs can be convoluted and confusing, especially in comparison to fuel costs. With tiered electricity pricing, time of use rates, demand charges, and potential interactions with solar and public charging, understanding the operating costs and payback time of EVs is complicated.

Using your fleet's own data and our sophisticated analytics, MyFleetBuy-EV makes it easy to compare the EV charging costs for your fleet.

Deployment plans to minimize risk and costs

Ready to move forward with buying EVs for your fleet? Get the most value out of your new vehicles. MyFleetBuy-EV helps maximize your cost savings with EVs by helping plan which of your fleet's routes and duty cycles are the best fit for your new EVs, while navigating the range and charging constraints.
